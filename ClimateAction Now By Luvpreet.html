About ClimateAction Now

ClimateAction Now was founded in 2010 by a group of concerned citizens who were passionate about taking action on climate change.

Since then, we have grown into a statewide organization with a team of dedicated staff and volunteers. We work to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, promote renewable energy, and protect Florida's natural resources.

We believe that everyone has a role to play in addressing climate change, and we are committed to empowering individuals, businesses, and communities to take action.

Our Work

We are working on a variety of projects to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and build a more sustainable future for Florida.

  • We are working to promote renewable energy.
  • We are working to protect Florida's natural resources.
  • We are working to educate the public about climate change.
  • We are working to empower individuals, businesses, and communities to take action.

We are committed to making a difference, and we believe that together we can create a more sustainable future for Florida.
